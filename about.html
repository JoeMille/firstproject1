About Us...

The Coastal Chef Team are a staple of Southern Coastal Chef hire.

Based in the West Country, our team of dedicated and reliable chefs serve all our clients to the highest professional standard.

All of our recruits are faced with an intense skills and knowledge test before interview and come from highly reputable professional backgrounds. Diverse in ability and drive, all of our partner chefs offer the highest in culinary standard, at an industry breaking rate.

Flexible and eager to assist in all manners of kitchen duty regardless of location, or duration.

Our agency was founded by former chefs with Michelin starred backgrounds, aiming to bring their own high level of efficiency and attention to detail to the world of hospitality. Our senior consultant has worked for some of the countries most highly regarded restaurants and hotels. After over a decade of service within the industry and on the front lines of the kitchen, The Coastal Chef Company was born; supplying the Uk's hardest working restaurants with talented and exceptional staff.
